About Us

Belfast Met is the largest and longest established further and higher education college in Northern Ireland. Founded in 1906, Belfast Met's mission has always been to improve the economic and social success of Belfast by educating the people, allowing them to gain the necessary skills for work.

To further improve the employability of Belfast, We offer a pathway to university, known as an Access Course. These courses aim to prepare future students with the knowledge and skills to complete a university degree.
