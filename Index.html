The Access to Higher Education (HE) Diploma at Belfast Metropolitan College is your gateway to university, providing essential knowledge and study skills to help you succeed. Open to all ages and backgrounds, this flexible, QAA-accredited qualification can be completed in one year or part-time.

Whether you're looking for a career change or a fresh start, this diploma is designed to prepare you for degree-level study and future success. With 60 credits (45 graded at Level 3), the program is tailored to your ambitions. Define your career goals, choose the right subjects, and select a study format that fits your life.
