Take A Tour Of Our Campuses

Belfast Met Offers Access Courses at two Campuses currently, those being the Titanic Quarter Campus, and the Millfield Campus. Take a tour of these Campuses below, as well as Belfast Met's e3 and Castlereagh Campuses

Millfield Campus

The Millfield Campus is located in the heart of Belfast. The Millfield Campus offers eight Access Courses, Validated by Ulster University and Queens University.

Titanic Quarter Campus

The Titanic Quarter Campus is the largest of the four campuses and is located in Titanic Quarter. This Campus offers six Access Courses, Validated by either Ulster University or Queens University.

Springvale Campus

The Springvale Campus is located on Springfield Road. While not offering Access Courses, it is home to the Belfast Film and Television School, equipped with state-of-the-art film production technology.

Castlereagh Campus

The Castlereagh Campus is located on the Castlereagh Road. While not offering Access Courses, the Castlereagh Campus has a great science department and courses, as well as a fantastic sports department.
